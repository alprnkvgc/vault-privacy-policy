# Privacy Policy – Vault

Vault is a local-first personal finance application. Your financial data is stored only on your device and is not shared with us.

## Data Collection
- Financial records are stored locally on your device.
- By default, analytics and crash reporting are disabled.
- If enabled by the user, anonymous usage metrics and error logs may be collected to improve the app.

## Third-Party Services
Vault may use the following third-party services:
- Google Firebase Analytics (optional)
- Google Firebase Crash Reporting (optional)

These services collect anonymous, aggregated data and do not include personally identifiable information.

## User Control
Users can enable or disable analytics and crash reporting at any time from the app settings.

## Data Security
All collected data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption.
